Hello. Recently, I’ve been concerned about the increase in the number of cars in our neighbourhood. 
This explosive increase is causing, air pollution, noise, and traffic accidents. I think we have to put a 
brake on this increase. To stop this increase, we have to know the reason why this increase is 
happening.  There are three reasons. First is the inconvenience of the metro. We have to propose some 
improvements of the metro system to the metro company, such as, ticket-buying systems, the 
number of trains, etc. If these problems will be solved, I’m pretty sure that the amount of traffic will 
be decreased. The second reason is the width of road. Most of the roads in this town are too narrow 
for cars to pass by. So, these narrow roads must be widened, in order to reduce amount of traffic. 
And the last reason is the fall in prices of gasoline. These days, the price of gasoline is falling so 
rapidly. I think the price of gasoline should be fixed in order to reduce this lorry traffic.  I hope metro companies and municipality will be checking my comment.
